Tribute to PLO Lumumba

Patrick Loch Otieno Lumumba ,famously known as PLO Lumumba is universally known for his unwavery commitment and dedication towards seeing a just and independent African continent.

Early Life

PLO Lumumba, born in November 7, 1975 in Ugenya Siaya county , Kenya,he came from a hand to mounth family.His parents, Raila and Ida relentlessly strived to bring him up to become a proper African man despite the challenges.

Despite the financial crisis,Lumumba managed to graduate from Harvard university with a first class degree honours in law.

Dedications

He has relentlessly championed against the neo colonisation and fought to uphold the African culture,transparency from Africanleaders from various countries.

PLO Lumumba with his eloquency of speech has managed to converge a number of great African leaders and spoke fearlessly on how they are misusing the power manifested upon them by the people.His power of speech has made him receive a standing ovation in all the countries he visits.

Achievements

He is the founder of the PLO Lumumba foundation which was established to help the African children from poor background .By so doing he has inspired the young generation to use their knowledge to bring positive positive change in Africa.

In 2002,He was awarded for pioneering in the peace keeping in South Africa which was almost ending up in flames.He has solemly acted as a pillar of hope in many African countries during their time of crisis

Thanksgiving

I celebrate my mentor a brilliant,a brilliant scholar and a true Afican icon.

PLO Lumumba's name name will forever be etched in the annals of African history as a symbol of justice.
